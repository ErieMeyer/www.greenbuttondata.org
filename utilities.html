If you are a Utility, Do want to provide Green Button Data for your customers?

Utilities are finding value in providing timely information to their customers. Getting started with Green Button is an essential first step to insure your investment in IT infrastructure will provide the best value to your customers. To get started with Green Button:

  1. Ask your current IT vendor if Green Button services are available in their product lines.
  2. If you build and maintain your own IT infrastructure, check out the Open Source Green Button Implementation. It is enterprise quality and scalable and may jumpstart you Green Button Support
  3. Purchase the North American Energy Standards Board Energy Services Providers Interface standard
