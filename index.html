The Green Button Initiative

White House inspired - Industry led - Green Button is the symbol for a consumer’s access to their own usage data. Green Button provides the ability to access data in a machine-readable format and to control who and where the data is sent. Green Button enables a consumer to do everything from printing a physical copy to sharing it with a third party application.
