If you are chartered to help consumers use resources more efficiently and think usage data will help your programs - Green Button is for you!

Your efficiency programs can gain additional value and relivance by using the actual metered data. You will be able to base-line actual consumption easiliy and provide your clients with the best possible guidance in achieving efficient usage of their energy and water resources. Green Button allows for daily, monthly, or annual interval data feeds and remain fully under the control of your client.
